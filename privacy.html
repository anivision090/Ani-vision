Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ani Vision. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your data when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

Ani Vision is a frontend-only website. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information. However, we may collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (for responsive design)
  • Anonymized usage statistics via cookies
  • Video viewing preferences (locally stored)

3. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your video playback position
  • Save your category preferences
  • Improve website performance
  • Remember if you've visited before

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

4. Video Content

All videos on Ani Vision are embedded from Google Drive. When you watch videos:

  • Google may collect data according to their privacy policy
  • We don't track which specific videos you watch
  • No viewing history is stored on our servers
  • Video analytics are handled by Google, not us

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Drive: For hosting video content
  • Font Awesome: For icons
  • Google Fonts: For typography
  • Cloudflare CDN: For content delivery

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them.

6. Data Protection

Since we don't collect personal data:

  • No user accounts are required
  • No email addresses are collected
  • No payment information is processed
  • No personal data is shared with third parties

7. Children's Privacy

Ani Vision is suitable for all ages. However:

  • We don't knowingly collect data from children under 13
  • Parents should monitor children's internet usage
  • Content is anime-focused but generally family-friendly
  • No registration means no age verification

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do:

  • The "Last Updated" date will be changed
  • We'll notify users via website banner
  • Continued use means acceptance of changes
  • Major changes will be highlighted

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy:

  • Email: privacy@anivision.example.com
  • Note: This is a demo website
  • No actual email service is configured
  • For real websites, use proper contact methods

10. Legal Compliance

This website complies with:

  • GDPR (for EU visitors)
  • CCPA (for California residents)
  • COPPA (for children's privacy)
  • Standard web privacy practices

Important Notice

This is a demonstration website created for educational purposes. This privacy policy is a template and should be reviewed by legal professionals for real-world use. Always ensure your website complies with local laws and regulations.
